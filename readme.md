# resource allocation through history ✨

**a living poster — [live at resource.gitcoin.co](https://resource.gitcoin.co)**

> 🖼️ **like it? [buy the physical poster](https://store.gitcoin.co/products/resource-allocation-history-framed-photo-paper-poster)** — the original "resource allocation through history" print, framed, from the gitcoin store.

An interactive remake of the Gitcoin "resource allocation through history" poster — 12,000 years of deciding *who gets what, and who decides*. Thirteen sibling posters are folded in as tabs: **[who decides](https://resource.gitcoin.co/power) · [how you eat](https://resource.gitcoin.co/live) · [what is true](https://resource.gitcoin.co/truth) · [who you are](https://resource.gitcoin.co/identity) · [who you trust](https://resource.gitcoin.co/trust) · [who keeps the record](https://resource.gitcoin.co/record) · [how you fight](https://resource.gitcoin.co/conflict) · [what can be proven](https://resource.gitcoin.co/math) · [what you owe](https://resource.gitcoin.co/ethics) · [what powers you](https://resource.gitcoin.co/energy) · [who heals you](https://resource.gitcoin.co/care) · [who owns the place](https://resource.gitcoin.co/land) · [who sets the clock](https://resource.gitcoin.co/time)** — potlatch to quadratic funding, chiefdoms to forkable states, songlines to zero-knowledge proofs, wergild to peace bonds, tally marks to validity proofs, firewood to local energy markets, the midwife to a wage for the caregiver, the commons to the land value tax, the monastery bell to a clock nobody owns. Each one explained.

Click around. It sparkles.

## features

- `index.html` — fourteen tabs, 988 mechanisms: **allocation** (81), **power structures** (80), **livelihood** (77), **energy** (76), **care** (75), **time** (73), **mathematics** (72), **land** (71), **ethics** (70), **truth** (68), **identity** (66), **trust** (60), **conflict** (60), **the record** (59)
- Every mechanism explained on click — what it is, where it came from, its tradeoffs
- Each era ends with the **unbuilt ones** ✦ — specs waiting for a builder
- **[what powers you](https://resource.gitcoin.co/energy)** is the other rate limiter: no city without the granary, no cathedral without the ox, no model without the gigawatt. It ends where Jevons left it — we have never, not once, used less
- **[who heals you](https://resource.gitcoin.co/care)**, **[who owns the place](https://resource.gitcoin.co/land)** and **[who sets the clock](https://resource.gitcoin.co/time)**: the healer migrates from kin to insurer and the largest care system on earth is still on nobody's budget; the land claim migrates from presence to token and every transfer, followed back, is a transfer from somebody who was there; the clock migrates from sun to block, and the future still gets exactly one vote — the discount rate
- **[what can be proven](https://resource.gitcoin.co/math)** is the rate limiter under all the others: no insurance before probability, no corporation before limited liability, no quadratic funding before somebody gave the square root a job. The advanced capital structures live here too — the commenda and the waqf, then limited liability and the share, then the derivative and the index fund, then the bonding curve and the hypercert
- **[what you owe](https://resource.gitcoin.co/ethics)** is the question that always arrives late. Its five hardest entries — the ones that were, in their own era, the ordinary and respectable view — sit behind a switch, off by default
- Every tab has its own crawlable URL + social card, so links unfurl properly
- Light & dark themes, responsive layout, reduced-motion friendly
- Self-contained HTML files — no build step, no dependencies

## running locally

It's just files. Open `index.html` in a browser, or serve the folder with anything:

```sh
python3 -m http.server
```
